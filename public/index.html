Biography

Timothy Ballard is a computational cognitive scientist in the School of Psychology at the University of Queensland. He is a member of the Dynamic Decision Making and Performance Lab, and is affilitated with the Centre for Business and Organisational Psychology and the Cognition, Mathematical Modelling, and Neuroscience Group.
